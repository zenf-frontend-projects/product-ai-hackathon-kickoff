There is a growing gap between what AI can do and what business can build.

03 / 07

A headspace gap, not a training gap.

The problem

  • PMs aren't using Claude day-to-day.
  • They need headspace, not training.
  • Grab, Singtel, HSBC all say the same: protected time is what works.

The solution

  • One day with no BAU, no sprint work.
  • Every PM ships something with Claude on a real squad problem.
  • Get the creative juices flowing, experiment.

Why you

  • Only works if POs lead it for their squad.
  • You know the workflows and leverage points.
  • We need POs to help bring energy, enablement and follow through!
04 / 07

What to build, how to build

The build

  • Building solo is recommended.
  • PMs should come up with their own problems to solve. But POs can recommend.
  • What to ship? An n8n agent, a skill library, a dashboard, a new tool — Anything that is useful
  • Use Claude, Claude Code, n8n. Experiment.

The rhythm

  • Morning kickoff to set the scene
  • All PM declare publicly what they are building
  • Midday squad demos (Show-and-tell)
  • Final squad demos at end of day

The demo

  • End of day: demo to your squad.
  • POs summarise demos, celebrate in the Slack channel.
  • Selected projects demo live later that week (Maybe?)

06 / 07

What you need to do as a Product Owner

Before 4 May

Set your PMs up for success.

  • Announce to your PMs this week. Get them thinking about real squad problems AI could solve.
  • Draft 1–2 problem statements yourself. Send them to Zen so he can stress-test fit, ambition, and scope.
  • Tell your Tribe Lead and Eng Lead: 4 May is blocked. PMs are 100% focused unless a P0 hits.
  • Protect the calendars. No BAU, no sprint ceremonies, no ad-hoc asks.
On the day

Lead, anchor, celebrate.

  • Every PM has a mission. Make sure no one is drifting by mid-morning.
  • Host the show-and-tell. Run the live check-ins. Unblock, guide, nudge.
  • Keep the energy up from kickoff to wrap. You set the temperature.
  • Close out loud. Encourage every PM to record a demo video and share in Slack so the whole org can watch on their own time.
